      About

      Located in Dundee The Little Green Larder offers something for everyone. With zero waste philosophies, customers are invited to bring their own containers to the shop, recycled and biodegradable packaging is also available. With a wide selection of vegetarian and vegan foods, customers can shop for locally sourced produce without having to worry about the effect on the environment.
